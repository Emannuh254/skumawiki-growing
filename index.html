Vegetable Growing Steps

  1. Seed Selection and Preparation: Choose high-quality seeds.
  2. Sowing: Plant seeds in a seedbed or tray.
  3. Transplanting: Move seedlings to the main garden bed.
  4. Weeding: Remove weeds manually or with a hoe.
  5. Compost Application: Enrich soil with organic matter.
  6. Watering: Keep soil moist for healthy growth.
  7. Pest Control: Use safe pesticides or natural remedies.
  8. Harvesting: Pick vegetables when mature.
